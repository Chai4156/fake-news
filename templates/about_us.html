Fakey

Our Mission

At FakeNews Detect, our mission is to combat misinformation and fake news by providing accessible, accurate, and fast fact-checking tools powered by state-of-the-art artificial intelligence and deep learning models.

What We Do

We provide an intelligent platform that analyzes news articles, headlines, and claims to determine their authenticity. Using advanced deep learning models trained on vast datasets of verified and false information, our system can quickly assess the credibility of news with high accuracy.

How It Works

Our system utilizes:

  • Deep Learning Models: Roberta and SBERT have trained for highest accuracy and nuances
  • Real-Time Data: Acessing websites in real time for for up-to-date info
  • Tri-Modal Dependency: three models have been integrated to enhance detection capabilities
  • Source Verification: Cross-referencing with reliable news sources

Why It Matters

In today's digital age, misinformation spreads rapidly. Fake news can influence public opinion, affect financial markets, and even compromise public health. Our tool helps individuals and organizations make informed decisions by providing evidence-based fact checks.

Our Team

Fakey is built by a team of passionate data scientists, machine learning engineers, committed to fighting misinformation and promoting media literacy.
